The others are larger versions of existing video: Katatedori Nikkyo Omote, Ryotedori Ikkyo Omote, Ryotedori Shihonage Omote, Shomenuchi Nikkyo Omote and Ura, Shomenuchi Sankyo Omote and Ura, Shomenuchi Shihonage Omote, Yokomenuchi Ikkyo Omote and Ura, and Yokomenuchi Shihonage Omote.

May 17, 2002 - Alexander Sensei has given me permission to post photos from his seminar in Sweden. They are now available for viewing in the Media section, under Seminar Images.

 

May 13, 2002 - A message from Alexander Sensei...

 

Dear Aikido students -
As some of you already know, Morihiro Saito-Sensei, my teacher and mentor, passed away last Sunday.
I will be at class this Wednesday and lead a prayer at the end.  We will schedule a gathering to celebrate his spirit at a future time.
Hope you can make it.
Dave.

 

May 12, 2002 - Morihiro Saito Shihan passes.
