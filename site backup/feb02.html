February 2, 2002 - A few last minute reminders for those attending the Koga seminar next weekend:
- You do not need to wear a gi. Koga Sensei trains in sweats and a t-shirt. You may wear your gi if you wish, but no hakama.
- Show up on time. Class will begin right at 11:30am on Saturday and right at 8:00am on Sunday.
- Although there is a water fountain in the dojo, it probably wouldn't hurt to have your own bottle of water handy.
- The Aikido party in Koga Sensei's honor will take place at 6pm after training on Saturday, at Alexander Sensei's house. A form with directions will be posted on this site very soon. Directions will also be available at the dojo.
- Even though the dojo will be catering the party with some good Bar-B-Q, it is pot luck, so it would be much appreciated if all seminar attendees would bring a drink, snack, or dessert item.
- Please continue to check this website all week for any last minute information that may arise. If you have any questions, feel free to email the webmaster.
Also, in case you missed the post late last month, pictures from the December Aikido Party are up in the Media section.
