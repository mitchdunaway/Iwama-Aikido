June 2002

 

June 26, 2002 - Thanks to everybody who turned out this past Wednesday. Your support is greatly appreciated!
Alexander Sensei will be teaching one night next week. Check back soon to find out what night.
Another copy of Saito Sensei's hard to find Traditional Aikido series is up on eBay. It's a copy of Volume 1 with the original cover style. Click here to view the auction. Westlake Village Aikido does not endorse the quality of the seller, just the quality of the book.

 

June 26, 2002 - There is no training on Thursday, July 4th. Classes will resume on Friday, July 5th.

 

June 24, 2002 - It has been confirmed, Alexander Sensei will be teaching class this coming Wednesday! This is an important training day so, if it is at all possible, please attend.

 

June 23, 2002 - Video footage was recorded today for the upcoming public access Traditional Aikido show. The only things missing are some shots from an actual Westlake Village Aikido training session. I intend to capture this missing footage this coming Wednesday, June 26th. I would obviously like to have the mat as filled with students as possible. It would be really helpful if all students enrolled (and some who haven't made it in a while) could show up for this class. As an added bonus, Alexander Sensei will be teaching the class!
Please come out to support your dojo. Your presence will be greatly appreciated!

 

June 20, 2002 - Additional photos from Alexander Sensei's seminar in Sweden have been posted on the Seminar Images page of the Media section.

 

June 19, 2002 - For those who attended any of Saito Sensei's seminars in America over the past 5 years or so, you may remember his translator, a gentleman named Mark Larson. Mark Larson was fortunate enough to study with Saito Sensei for nine years. He has written a tribute to Saito Sensei and posted it on aikidojournal.com. I have provided a link here. I thought it was really nice remembrance. True to the Way.

 

June 18, 2002 - Bryan Kurata has been okayed for 4th kyu and added to the testing list.

 

June 16, 2002 - A group photo has been added to the collection of images from Alexander Sensei's seminar in Sweden. This can be found on the Seminar Images page of the Media section.
